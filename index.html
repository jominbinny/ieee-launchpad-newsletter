Welcome to the official newsletter for the IEEE Launchpad Kerala 2024 event. This newsletter provides essential information about the event, including its purpose, schedule, and how to participate.

Introduction

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to create meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market.

Why Join Us?

Whether you're a job seeker looking for exciting opportunities or a company seeking fresh talent, Launchpad Kerala 2024 is the place to be. This event is designed to facilitate networking, learning, and growth for all participants.
